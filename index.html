Hello there,

My name is Austin Keener. I specialize in Web Development as well as Graphic and Web Design. I really enjoy what technology has to offer, and look forward to contributing to the ever expanding world wide web.

I chose to expand my knowledge of Web Development by attending the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp in 2020 which couples with my degree in Graphic and Web Design. This has made me proficient with Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and other popular Design software on the internet. I also have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Node.

I use my knowlege of design and development to adapt to the work that I am given. I do my best to make each customer's experience unique by incorporating a single visual voice using color, type, and imagery, throughout each composition that I create.
