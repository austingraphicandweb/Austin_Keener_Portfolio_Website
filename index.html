My name is Austin Keener. I am 26 years old. I was born and raised in Marietta, Georgia. I received my degree in Graphic and Web Design this past March of 2020. I really enjoy what technology has to offer, and look forward to contributing to the ever expanding www.

As a hardworking, adaptive, understanding, and thorough creative with roughly ten years helping others in the customer service industry I currently work as a Graphic Designer and Web Developer as a freelancer and am also enrolled in the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp. I have a professional degree in Graphic and Web Design which has made me proficient in Adobe illustrator, inDesign, and Photoshop. I also have experience with HTML, css, javascript, and jQuery. I like to adapt to my work environment by tailoring each customer's experience to be unique which means that I incorporate a single visual voice using color, type, and imagery, throughout each composition that I create.
